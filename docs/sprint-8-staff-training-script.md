# Sprint 8 — Namotu front-desk staff training script

**Format:** Text for a 1–2 page printed handout. Designed to be read once, kept at the desk, and torn at the perforation if the team only wants the summary card.
**Audience:** Namotu Island Fiji front-desk and reception team.
**Pilot window:** Q2 2026 soft launch. Namotu is the only property live on Koncie.

---

## What Koncie is

Koncie is the new trip-hub app your guests get access to after they book. It gives them one place online to see their booking, read pre-arrival info, and pre-book activities and travel insurance before they land in Nadi.

## What changes for your guests

Almost nothing changes. They still book their room through the usual channels, and that booking still flows through HotelLink the same way it always has. The only new thing they'll notice is an email that arrives shortly after their booking is confirmed, with the subject line "Your trip hub is ready". That email contains a single button — when they click it, they're signed straight into their personal trip hub on Koncie. From there they can review their booking, work through a short pre-arrival checklist, and browse Namotu's activities and travel-insurance options ahead of time. Anything they pre-book lands on their itinerary so it's all in one place when they arrive.

## What changes for you

Honestly, very little. Room bookings keep flowing through HotelLink as they do today, and the room folio is still yours to manage at the desk. The only difference is that some guests will arrive having already pre-paid for activities through their trip hub. Those activity bookings appear on the daily activities manifest the same way as any other pre-paid booking — they just have "Koncie" listed as the source instead of a phone or email reservation. Treat them the way you'd treat any other pre-paid activity: name on the list, gear ready, smile on. You don't need to take any payment from the guest at the desk for anything they've already booked through Koncie.

If a guest also bought travel insurance through their trip hub, that's handled entirely between Koncie and CoverMore. The desk has nothing to do, no paperwork to chase, no policy numbers to file. The guest already has their policy in their inbox.

## What to do if a guest asks about Koncie

**"What is this Koncie email I got?"**
It's the trip hub for your stay with us. Kovena built it so guests can see their booking and pre-book activities and travel insurance in one place. The link in the email signs you straight in — no password to remember. If you'd rather just sort everything when you arrive, that's completely fine, you don't have to use it.

**"Can I just book activities at the desk instead?"**
Absolutely, nothing has changed there. The desk can book any activity for you the way we always have. Koncie is just an option for guests who like to plan ahead before they fly in.

**"Something's wrong with my Koncie account / I can't log in / my booking isn't showing."**
Apologise for the hiccup, reassure them their room booking and any activities they've already paid for are absolutely safe on our side, and let them know you'll flag it with the Kovena team. Take a quick note of their name, email, and what went wrong, and pass it to the contact below. In the meantime, book whatever they need at the desk as normal — Koncie working or not, their holiday goes ahead unaffected.
